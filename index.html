My Journey

Nearly a decade ago, I joined Code Ninjas, where I progressed through the curriculum and earned my Black Belt in programming. Following my experience in the programming world, I wanted to explore other areas of computer science. That is when I tried cybersecurity at GenCyber, and I fell in love! After three consecutive years of attending GenCyber, I decided to take my skills to the next level and join a cybersecurity team. After my first year on the team, I wanted to apply my skills to the workforce, so I applied for a MidAmerican Energy Cybersecurity Internship. I was one of the few to be accepted into the program, and the only high schooler out of hundreds to be accepted into the Cybersecurity-specific internship! I continue to learn and grow in the cybersecurity field, and I am excited to see where my journey takes me next.
